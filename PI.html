About the PI

Tomer Galanti is an Assistant Professor of Computer Science at Texas A&M University. Previously, I was a postdoctoral associate at MIT's Center for Brains, Minds & Machines (CBMM), collaborating with Tomaso Poggio. I received my Ph.D. in computer science from Tel Aviv University, where I worked with Lior Wolf. In the summer of 2021, I served as a Research Scientist Intern with the Foundations team at Google DeepMind, where I worked with Andras Gyorgy and Marcus Hutter.
