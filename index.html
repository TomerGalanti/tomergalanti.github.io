About

Howdy! We are the Deep Learning Fundamentals group at Texas A&M, led by Prof. Tomer Galanti. We combine theory and experiments , our research develops realistic models of contemporary learning settings to guide best practices in deep learning and LLMs.

Research

Representation learning

Structure and dynamics of learned features across depth and modalities; how they drive generalization and transfer.

Neural collapse Transfer learning theory SSL clustering & alignment

LLM theory & algorithms

Optimization, inference, and learning dynamics of large language models; with collaborators incl. Tianbao Yang, Eran Malach, and Nadav Timor.

Speculative inference Structured LMs (ADT) Fair LM paradox

AI for economics

Bringing representation learning and LLM tools to economic modeling and analysis.
